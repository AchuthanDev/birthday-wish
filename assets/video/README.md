# Custom Video

Place your custom BTS fan edit video here.

## Instructions

1. Add your video file to this folder
2. Name it `your-video.mp4` (or `your-video.webm`)
3. Supported formats: MP4, WebM, OGG
4. Recommended resolution: 1920x1080 or 1280x720
5. Recommended file size: Under 50MB for faster loading

## How it works

- When the gift box is opened, your custom video will appear in the video section
- When your video plays, the background YouTube music will automatically pause
- When your video is paused or ends, the background music will resume

## Note

If no video is found, the section will show a placeholder message. Make sure to add your video file to see it in action!
